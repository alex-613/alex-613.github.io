Hi I'm Alex!

This is page that is dedicated to the projects that I have worked on as a mechanical engineer and a developer. My ethos as an engineer is to develop systems that changes lives in a positive way.
As a result, I became most passionate about Medical Technologies, Robotics and Automation, as well as XR. Below are a selection of projects that I have worked on, and I am excited to share them with you!

Neural network for predicting ablation path for LiTT (Coming Soon)

LiTT stands for laser interstitial thermal therapy, and it is a procedure used to treat epilepsy. Partnering with the EDEN 2020 project, a neural net is being developed to find the optimal path for a soft robot needle to minimize thermal damage.

OSMR

OSMR stands for open source medical robot, and is an initiative that aims to create a collaborative and low cost tendon driven continuum robot for endoscopic surgery.

AR for malleable robot

A robot with rigid links cannot possible reach everywhere in a workspace due to singularities and other constraints. But what about a malleable robot?

Signature Robot

Performing accurate knee replacement (Arthoplasty) is a dangerous operation in orthopaedic surgeries. This can be mitigated via a cleverly designed robot that utilizes active constraints, and small wrist motions, just like signing with a pen.

UAV for bomb disposal

Final project for my course on Embedded C for Microcontrollers course, used to difuse a hypothetical bomb at Imperial.

Mechatronics Labs via Simulink

The penguin

Who doesnt love a penguin looking race car? ;)

Electric UniWheel

This is my first ever project that utilizes mechanical and flexible transmissions to transmit energy from a motor to a wheel.
